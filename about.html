About Us

Founded in 2005, Vaagdevi Junior & Degree College focuses on empowering rural youth with accessible, high-quality education and values-based learning.

We offer undergraduate degrees, intermediate programs, and a Defence Academy wing, guided by experienced faculty and a student-first approach.
